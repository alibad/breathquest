Technical Approach

üé§ Phase 1: Microphone

Web Audio API for real-time breath detection. FFT analysis on low frequencies. Smoothing algorithms to filter noise.

üì° Phase 2: Sensors

Bluetooth chest straps for precise respiratory rate. WebBluetooth API integration. Heart rate variability correlation.

ü§ñ Phase 3: AI Coaching

LLM-powered breath coach. Pattern recognition for technique improvement. Personalized difficulty curves.

üåê Phase 4: Multiplayer

WebRTC for synchronized breathing sessions. Collaborative breath puzzles. Global breath meditation events.

Core Hypotheses

  • Hypothesis 1: Mic-based breath detection is accurate enough for gameplay (no special hardware needed for MVP)
  • Hypothesis 2: Visual feedback synchronized with breath creates stronger awareness than audio cues alone
  • Hypothesis 3: Gamification drives better habit formation than traditional meditation apps
  • Hypothesis 4: Breath patterns can become a unique biometric signature for personalized AI interactions
  • Hypothesis 5: Multiplayer breath synchronization creates stronger social bonds than traditional online gaming
